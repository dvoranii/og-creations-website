About Us

OG Creations is a Toronto-based recording studio that opened its doors in March of 2019. We offer a variety of recording, music production, and mixing and mastering services to meet the needs of new and upcoming artists across the Greater Toronto Area. Our team is experienced, knowledgeable, and talented when it comes to music production and we can help you get your music out into the world. Our goal at OG Creations is to help new, upcoming artists, or experienced recording artists create a name for themselves in the music industry. We work hard to help promote our clients so that they can continue to get more recognition for their talents. Learn more about our services and contact us to get started!
